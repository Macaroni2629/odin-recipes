Hamburger Recipe

This recipe serves a family of 4!

Ingredient List

  • Hamburger Meat, Raw
  • Hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of American cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Lettuce

Directions

  1. Cook hamburger meat on stove.
  2. Place cheese slices on top of hamburger meat while meat is still on stove.
  3. After cheese is partially melted, use spatula to place hamburger patty on toasted buns.
  4. Place ketchup.
  5. Place pickles.
  6. Place mustard.
  7. Close hamburger bun.
  8. Enjoy!
