Delicious Cheesecake Recipe

This is a delicious cheesecake that your family will love.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Cherries
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Mix flour and water and eggs.
  2. Mix cheese.
  3. Leave mixture in refrigerator for 3 hours.
  4. Add 4 cups of water.
  5. Place entire mixture in refrigerator for 4 hours.
