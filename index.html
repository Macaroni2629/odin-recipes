You're going to love how this lasagna tastes.

Ingredients

  • onions
  • tomatoes
  • cheese
  • pasta
  • pasta sauce

This delicious lasagna recipe is made for people who are not vegetarians unfortunately.

If you want a vegetarian version, please go to another web site to find a vegetarian version.

Steps to Make Pasta

  1. Boil pasta in water.
  2. Chop onions.
  3. Mix onions into pasta sauce.
  4. Layer cheese with pasta with noodles.
  5. Place pan of lasagna into oven at 350 degrees.
